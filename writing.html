      About the 2026 Writing Section

      • Writing section lasts approximately 23 minutes total.
      • "Build a Sentence" and "Write an Email" each take about 7 minutes.
      • "Write for an Academic Discussion" takes about 10 minutes.
      • Writing tasks are likely scored by AI on the actual test.
