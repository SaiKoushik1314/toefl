Frequently Asked Questions

What changed in the TOEFL 2026 format? +
The TOEFL iBT changed on January 21, 2026. Reading now includes Complete the Words, Reading in Daily Life, and Academic Reading. Writing now includes Build a Sentence, Write an Email, and Write for an Academic Discussion.
Is this TOEFL practice site completely free? +
Yes, 100% free. No signup, no account, no credit card. The site is supported by ads to keep it free forever.
How long is the new TOEFL 2026 test? +
The Reading section is approximately 30 minutes and the Writing section is approximately 23 minutes. The full test including Listening and Speaking takes around 2 hours.
What is the Build a Sentence task? +
You see scrambled words and must drag and drop them into the correct order. Sometimes one extra word is included that should not be used. You get 1 point only for a perfectly correct sentence.
What is the Complete the Words task? +
You read an academic paragraph where 10 words have their second half hidden. You type the missing letters. It tests academic vocabulary. The Academic Word List is great preparation.
How long should my email be for Write an Email? +
Aim for 130-140 words. Cover all three required points, use polite formal language, and organize into two clear paragraphs.
