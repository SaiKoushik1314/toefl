What's New in TOEFL 2026?

The TOEFL iBT changed completely on January 21, 2026. Here's what you need to know.

📖

Reading – New Tasks

New adaptive format with "Complete the Words," "Reading in Daily Life," and shorter Academic passages. 30 minutes total.

✍️

Writing – New Tasks

"Build a Sentence" (word reordering), "Write an Email," and "Write for an Academic Discussion." 23 minutes total.

⚡

Shorter & Adaptive

The test is now shorter overall. Reading uses a routing module — your performance determines which second module you get.
