      📋 About the 2026 Reading Section

      • The reading section uses an adaptive format: a routing module followed by either an easy or hard module.
      • You can move backward and forward within a module, but not between modules.
      • Aim to correctly answer ~60% of routing questions to reach the hard module.
      • Some questions are unscored — you won't know which ones.
