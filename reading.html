      About the 2026 Reading Section

      • "Complete the Words" and "Reading in Daily Life" each take about 7 minutes.
      • "Academic Reading" takes about 10 minutes per passage.
      • The reading section uses an adaptive routing module.
      • Topics cover science, society, environment, psychology, and technology.
